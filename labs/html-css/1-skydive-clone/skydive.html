    How do we structure a day?

    1. Training

    We teach all the necessary things to jump from the plane without any kind of problem.

    We are already prepared, you just need the suit and a parachute. All sizes available.

    3. Fly

    You are ready and the plane is waiting for us at the hangar. Let's fly!

    4. Jump

    You have done the most complicated. Just one step left... Jump!
